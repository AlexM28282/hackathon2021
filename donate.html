UNICEF Kind Schools Projects

Unicef kind schools stands for Kids In Need of Desks. It's a fundraising program run by Unicef Canada, with a goal of providing supplies to 70+ million children who don't currently have access to education. We strongly to take a further look into this awesome fundraiser, and to consider donating to those who really need it
